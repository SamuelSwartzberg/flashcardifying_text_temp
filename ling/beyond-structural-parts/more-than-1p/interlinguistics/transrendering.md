# transrendering

## source and target

»⟮The source⟯« is ⟮the original material⟯ of ⟮＿a transrendering＿⟯.
»⟮The target⟯« is ⟮the result⟯ of ⟮＿a transrendering＿⟯.
»⟮The transrendering constant⟯« (my term) is what is ⟮being attempted to⟯ ⟮kept the same⟯ in ⟮＿a transrendering＿⟯.
»⟮The target/source medium⟯« is ⟮the medium⟯ of ⟮＿the target/source＿⟯.
^e.g. the target language is the target medium of translation.

## trans

»⟮Transrendering⟯« (my term) is ⟮the transformation⟯ of ⟮＿the transrendering constant＿⟯ ⟮c-;from⟯ the ⟮＿source medium＿⟯ ⟮c-;to⟯ ⟮＿the target medium＿⟯.

### translation

!»⟮Translation⟯« is ⟮＿transrendering＿⟯ where
!- transrendering constant: ⟮meaning⟯
!- source medium: ⟮language a⟯
!- target medium: ⟮language b⟯
^e.g. これは恋なの？ → Is this love?

### common transcription

!»⟮common transcription⟯« (my term) is ⟮＿transrendering＿⟯ where
!- transrendering constant: ⟮utterances⟯
!- source medium: ⟮spoken language⟯
!- target medium: ⟮written language⟯
^e.g. &lt;example not possible in written form alone&gt;

### orthographic transcription

!»⟮orthographic transcription⟯« is ⟮＿transrendering＿⟯ where

### transliteration
